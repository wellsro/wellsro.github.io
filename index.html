design philosophy

We often forget a few things!

How powerful our web presence is and how much of an effect it has on the users of the web presence which will then ripple out into the entire organizational function:

  • Our business success
  • Our market share
Everything can be affected by our web presence and we often get so focused on ourselves and our product we forget the most important thing which is our users. And effect web design has to be all about your users, it can't be about you. It is about them the users!

work ethic

Web development is not for everyone!

Almost all companies have some sort of web presence, website, social media, and online sales. As a web developer, you will be making decisions on what content is there what it looks like what channels you use, what marketing and branding you use, and what strategy your user experience design can all come down to you.

How can you be a leader in your organization and in their web presence, how can you make smart decisions about web presence for your business?

Focusing on HTML5 and CSS3 are not that bad to work with and don't bite that hard. They are pretty well-behaved languages. On the plus side, you will be building something and that is incredibly fun.

Love the feeling of building something that really works and functions and that is pretty cool.

Always cheerful and enjoy teaching.
